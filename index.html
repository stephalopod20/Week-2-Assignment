Welcome to Waxperiment!

Recently, I've been trying to be more aware of the trash I generate. I know it is important to reuse, made popular by the adage "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle". That left me with a problem: too many mostly-empty jars which used to house old candles and desperately needed a new use. Cue my only idea: using the last bits of wax in my oil burner!




So then I'm faced with a similarly maddening dilemma: what do I do with that leftover wax?


Some Thoughts on Reusing Candle Wax
Option 1 Option 2 Option 3
Melt it all into a new candle with a new wick (ensuring it's not a toxic wick, or toxwick) Add tons more essential oil Beg for candle companies to instill a take-back program to reuse their own jars and/or wax!

Lucky for us, thanks to Option 3, I know of at least one local brand that does this!
